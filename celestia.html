Celestia is the 6th World that your young wizard encounters on the their journey. The world is heavily based on the fictional greek society of Atlantis. This is very evident as the whole zone is underwater, and by many of the native creatures of the world you interact with. As most NPC's native to this world are animals that are found underwater.



Celestia is a world that for the most part is a step up in difficulty from the previous world Dragonspyre. This is the first world that introduces a new mechanic to boses which will become a staple in many of the worlds that proceed Celestia and that is boss cheats. While in Celestia the only boss cheat is the final boss and it is not a difficult cheat to deal with. Those who are not well equipped and playing with friends might encounter their dfirst trouble with this boss.
