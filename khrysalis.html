Khrysalis is the the 10th world your young wizard will encounter on their journey. This world is one of the more diverse zones in the games and also one of the biggest, having 13 different and unique zones and a quest tree that last 277 quests!



Khrysalis is arguably one of the most difficult worlds in all of Wizard101. This is mainly due to the amount of cheating boss battles you encounter on your journey throughout the world. Not only are there are a lot of cheating bosses throughout the world, some of these boss fights can include up to 4 cheating bosses at once. This can create havoc on wizards who are not geared up correctly and not playing with another group of wizards.
